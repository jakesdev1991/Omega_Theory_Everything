# Emergent Reality from Quantum Correlation: Life IS Quantum
## A Layman's Guide to the Omega Theory

**Jacob See**

### Abstract
Reality does not begin with matter or spacetime—it begins with correlation. Every piece of the universe is a quantum system sharing information with others. When those correlations thin, space appears to stretch; when they tighten, gravity deepens.

---

## 1. Building Reality, One Dimension at a Time

Where does our universe come from? It starts with a single point of view.

### The First Dimension (1D): The Observer
The first "dimension" is the observer in a state of unadulterated superposition. We call this a "Q-region." It is not located in space; it is a location in its own right. Consciousness is simply the ability to retain and communicate information.

### The Second Dimension (2D): The Relationship
Reality cannot exist in 1D. A lone observer sees nothing. A "real" event only happens when a second frame of reference observes the first. This "view of a view" collapses superposition into a definite outcome.

### The Third Dimension (3D): Time as Rate
Time is not a river we move down. It is a rate: **Time = d(state) / d(perspective)**. It measures how quickly information changes relative to the observer. A clock doesn't count an external "second"; it counts its own internal informational updates.

### The Fourth Dimension (4D): Mass is "The Difference"
This is the physical world we touch. In the Omega Theory (v3.4), Mass is defined by **Non-Overlap**.
* Imagine two circles of information. If they overlap perfectly, they become one thing (a Singularity).
* If they are slightly offset, the part that *sticks out*—the part that doesn't overlap—is what we call **Matter**.
* **Matter is the "Error" in the overlap.** It is the unique characteristic generated when symmetry breaks.

## 2. The Informational Field
Space is just a map of how much information regions share.
* **High Gravity** means high information sharing (short distance).
* **Empty Space** means low information sharing (long distance).
Gravity isn't a force pulling things; it's just information being very dense.

## 3. Cosmic Shredders: The Engine of Expansion
Why is the universe expanding? Not because of "Dark Energy," but because of **Black Holes**.
A Black Hole is a "Correlation Shredder." It pulls in information and cuts the connection to the outside. When you cut the connections that hold space together, the remaining space has to stretch. The universe is expanding because it is losing its internal memory.

## 4. Life: The Master of the Shredder
The universe shreds information passively (Entropy). Life shreds information *actively* (Metabolism).
A cell breaks down sugar (shredding correlations) to release energy, then uses that energy to build new, complex structures (DNA, proteins). Life is a machine that learned to swim upstream against the cosmic flow of information loss.
